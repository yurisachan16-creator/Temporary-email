# TempMail+ Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-03-28_

## Data We Collect

TempMail+ does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data generated by this extension is stored **locally** in your browser using `browser.storage.local`:

| Data | Purpose | Stored Where |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Temporary email addresses | Display your active mailboxes | browser.storage.local (local only) |
| User preferences (theme, language) | Remember your settings | browser.storage.local (local only) |

## Third-Party Services

TempMail+ fetches emails using public APIs from:

- **1secmail** (https://www.1secmail.com) - Primary email provider
- **mail.tm** (https://api.mail.tm) - Fallback email provider

When you generate a temporary email or check your inbox, your request goes directly from your browser to these services. Please review their privacy policies for how they handle API requests.

## Data Retention

- Temporary email addresses are stored until you discard them or clear browser storage.
- No data is ever sent to the extension developer's servers.
