# TempMail+ - Store Listing (English)

## Extension Name
TempMail+

## Short Description (<=132 characters)
Generate disposable email addresses instantly. Protect your real email from spam, with auto-fill and dark mode support.

## Full Description

**TempMail+** lets you create temporary email addresses in one click - no registration required.

### Key Features
- **Instant generation** - Get a working disposable email in under 1 second
- **Dual provider** - Automatically falls back from 1secmail to mail.tm on errors
- **Smart domain selection** - Prefers low-profile domains to reduce blocking
- **Domain reputation badges** - See if a domain is 🟢 rare / 🟡 medium / 🔴 common
- **Auto-fill** - Detects email input fields on any page and fills them for you
- **Multi-mailbox** - Manage up to 5 temporary inboxes simultaneously
- **Auto-polling** - Inbox refreshes every 10 seconds automatically
- **Dark mode** - Follow system theme or switch manually
- **Multi-language** - Simplified Chinese and English, auto-detected

### Use Cases
- Sign up for a service without exposing your real email
- QA/developer batch registration testing
- Read a newsletter once without subscribing permanently

### Privacy
All data is stored locally in your browser (browser.storage.local). No email content is ever uploaded to our servers. Emails are fetched directly from the mail provider's public API.
